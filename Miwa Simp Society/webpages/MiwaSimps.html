Welcome to Miwa Simps Society!


Logo created by: Fibel


Miwa Simps Society started as a small fans club for miwa by Jemuel and his friends. It started when they took a pic with miwa on Cosplay Matsuri 2022 Day 3 and felt stunned by her cosplay as Suisei, as everyone in the fans club is a huge Hoshiyomi. The fans club is currently led by Jemuel as per nomination of the members of the fans club.
